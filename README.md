# Amar Deen

**Amar Deen** ("My Religion") is a free, offline-first Islamic companion app for daily practice — prayer, Quran, remembrance, and more, all in one place, working without an internet connection.

Available in **English** and **বাংলা (Bangla)**.

## Features

### Prayer
- Accurate daily prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) calculated on-device — no internet required
- Choice of calculation method and madhab (Hanafi/Shafi'i)
- Live countdown to the next prayer, with the current prayer's start/end window
- Qibla direction finder, using your phone's compass
- Azan (call to prayer) notifications, with a toggle per prayer
- Voluntary prayer times: Ishraq, Duha, Tahajjud
- Ramadan mode: Suhoor/Iftar/Imsak times with a live countdown
- Voluntary fasting reminders: Mondays & Thursdays, Ayyam al-Beed (the "White Days"), and the Day of Arafah

### Quran
- Complete Quran text, browsable by Surah or Juz
- Verse-by-verse and full-Surah audio recitation, with a choice of reciters
- Adjustable Quran and translation font size/family, and translation language
- Bookmark and favorite individual verses
- Automatically resumes where you last left off
- In-app search across Surahs and Juz

### Azkar & Dua
- Daily remembrances (Azkar) organized by category and chapter, in multiple languages
- Qur'an-based supplications (Dua), with translations
- Favorite/bookmark your most-used Azkar and Dua

### Tasbih
- Digital tasbih counter with a customizable target count
- Haptic feedback on each tap and when you reach your target
- Save and reuse your own named counters

### Other tools
- **99 Names of Allah**, with meanings
- **Zakat calculator**, supporting cash, gold/silver, business assets, and debts
- **Live TV**, streaming Islamic channels
- A unified **Bookmarks** screen collecting your favorited Quran verses, Duas, and Azkar in one place
- Daily reminder notification to encourage a moment of reading or reflection
- Light and dark theme
- 12-hour/24-hour time format toggle

## Platforms

- Android (minimum SDK 16 / Android 4.1+)
- iOS (15.0+)

## Getting the app

Debug APK builds are published automatically under [Releases](../../releases) for each tagged version.

## Privacy

Amar Deen is offline-first: prayer times, Quran, Azkar, Dua, and Tasbih all work without a network connection. Location is used only to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction, and is never sent anywhere.
